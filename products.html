SOFT COMMODITIES


Our soft commodities division trades in food products such as Nuts and Legumes. Our main source of supply is from USA, Australia and Turkey with customers in India, Bangladesh, China and the Middle East.

We work in conjunction with Arissa International, a company established in Mumbai by a management team with more than two decades of experience in the soft commodities trade. We work closely with farmers and co-operatives, to ensure the most efficient and reliable supply chain. With an extensive network of strategically placed storage and handling facilities we take pride in ensuring the freshest produce of the highest standards for our customers.

Further details may be viewed at: www.foodstudio.co.in
